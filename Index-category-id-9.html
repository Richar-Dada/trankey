Regulatory guarantee

      Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission, CySEC. CySEC is an independent public watchdog. The agency oversees the trading of investment services markets and negotiable securities within the republic of Cyprus.

      The Cyprus securities and exchange commission was established under the securities and exchange commission act 2001. Its mission is to implement effective regulation to ensure the healthy development of the securities market and protect the investors involved in the market.

Registered within the territory of Cyprus, if provides investment services to a third party, or for their investment activities, must apply to the securities and exchange commission of Cyprus, and get a CIF (Chinese investment company to Cyprus) certification, can conduct business.

    (1). wide scope of jurisdiction

       As a government department in Cyprus, CySEC has a wide range of jurisdiction. There are mainly three parts:

       1, as a consumer credit regulators, CySEC authorized enterprise engaged in the consumer credit activities (including Banks, credit cooperatives, financial institutions, etc.), and ensure the license validity under the relevant specification, including the responsibility of the consumer.

       2. As the market regulator, CySEC evaluates how to effectively authorize the financial market, and evaluates the fair, orderly and transparent operation of the financial market in accordance with its legal obligations. CySEC, under the permission of the Cypriot government, is responsible for regulating the normal operation of the stock, derivatives and domestic futures markets that are permitted to trade in the market.

       3. As a financial services regulator, CySEC permits and monitors financial services companies to ensure their effective, honest and fair operation. These businesses cover pension insurance, funds, stocks, and corporate securities, derivatives, and insurance services.

    (2).Power in the financial market

       Provision of relief from legislative provisions managed by CySEC

       Maintain and manage information that is accessible to companies, licensed financial services and licensed credit holders

       Rules to ensure the integrity of financial markets

       To issue notices of infringement to individuals or groups suspected of violating the relevant laws

       Refuse to violate the rules, illegal personnel continue to engage in credit business or provide financial services
