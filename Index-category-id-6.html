Anti-money laundering policy:

     Money laundering is illegal (terrorism, drug trafficking, illegal arms trade, corruption, human trafficking, etc.) to get money or valuable material into a legitimate to invest money or legal behavior. The act would make it impossible to trace the source of money or other valuable substances illegally obtained.

     In order to deal with the infiltration of illegal funds into the state economy and prevent the expansion of terrorist activities, all countries are combating money laundering and combating terrorist financing. Financial institutions are one of the easiest and most convenient ways to turn illegally obtained money into legitimate income. The increasing integration of financial markets and the free flow of funds make it easy for illegal funds to penetrate capital markets. Therefore, the company will implement relevant measures in accordance with applicable laws and procedures to help international organizations combat money-laundering and terrorist financing activities around the world.

    1. The company records and verifies the customer's identity information, and records and tracks all transactions executed by the customer.

    2. The company will track suspicious transactions of customers and transactions executed under non-standard conditions. The company carries out its own activities on the recommendation of AML DATF.

    3.The company will not accept cash deposits or cash payments under any circumstances.

    4. The company has the right to refuse to handle a transaction at any time when it is certain that it is connected with money laundering or criminal activities. Under international law, companies are under no obligation to inform customers that their suspicious activities have been reported to the appropriate authorities.

    According to the latest rules, Trankey hengfeng will work to update its electronic systems, suspicious transactions, and customer authentication records on a regular basis. The company also provides staff training to strengthen resistance to money laundering to meet the new regulations.
